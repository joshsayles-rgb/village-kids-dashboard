Village Kids Dashboard
Volunteer Guide
Welcome
This guide will help you get the most out of the Village Kids Dashboard on Sunday mornings.
Getting Started
Opening the Dashboard
Open Safari on your iPad and go to your room's bookmark, or tap the Village Kids icon on your home screen if it's been added.
1
Open the app on your room's iPad
2
You'll see your room's schedule already selected
3
The green dot in the top right means you're live and synced
The Countdown Timer
The dark bar at the top of the page shows a live countdown to the next event in your room's schedule. It switches automatically between 1st and 2nd service.
Your Volunteer Team
Scroll down to see who's scheduled in your room for each service — names, roles, and confirmation status pulled live from Planning Center.
Sending a Room Alert
When to Send an Alert
Use the red Send alert to admin button at the bottom of the page when you need immediate help. Choose from:
I need another volunteer
Bathroom break needed
Medical situation — send help immediately
General — I need help
What Happens Next
The admin iPad will immediately display a red banner with your alert and play a chime. Someone from the coordination team will come to your room. You'll see a confirmation message after sending.
Other Features
Class Schedule
Your room's full schedule for the morning is listed in the Class schedules card — including event times, worship, lesson, and pickup. The current activity is highlighted automatically.
Curriculum Link
If your room has a curriculum folder linked for the week, you'll see an Open curriculum folder button at the top of your class schedule. Tap it to access lesson materials directly.
Announcements
Important updates from the kids ministry team appear at the top of the page. Check these at the start of each service for any last-minute changes.
Dark Mode
Tap the moon icon in the top right corner to switch to dark mode — easier on the eyes in dimly lit rooms.
Need Encouragement?
Kids ministry is hard work! Tap the green Need encouragement? button for a joke or Bible verse to brighten your morning.
Sunday Report
After service, tap Submit Sunday report to fill out the weekly debrief form. It takes about 2 minutes and helps the team follow up with families.
💡
The dashboard refreshes automatically every 60 seconds and checks for alerts every 5 seconds. If you ever see a red dot in the top right corner, tap Refresh to sync immediately.
